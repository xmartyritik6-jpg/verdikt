About Verdikt

Verdikt exists because most product review sites make buying decisions harder, not easier. Star ratings get inflated, sponsored listings blend into "top picks," and real trade-offs get buried under marketing language. We built Verdikt to fix that for Indian shoppers comparing earbuds, smartphones, laptops, and smartwatches.

What we actually do

Every product on Verdikt gets a Trust Score — a rating built from three things: whether the product delivers on its advertised claims, real-world durability signals, and value for the price paid. It is not based on review volume or star counts, which are easy to inflate.

We also write out honest pros and cons for every product — including the trade-offs a brand's own marketing won't mention — and track price history so you can tell a genuine discount from an inflated "was" price.

How we make money

Verdikt uses affiliate links — if you buy a product through a link on this site, we may earn a small commission from the retailer (such as Amazon or Flipkart) at no extra cost to you. This never affects a product's Trust Score or its place in our rankings. Any sponsored placement, if introduced in future, will always be clearly labeled as such.

A work in progress

Verdikt is a new, independently built platform — not affiliated with Apple, Samsung, Xiaomi, OnePlus, Vivo, boAt, Noise, Fire-Boltt, or any other brand featured here. Product specs and prices are checked at the time of writing but can change — always confirm current price and availability on the retailer's page before buying.
